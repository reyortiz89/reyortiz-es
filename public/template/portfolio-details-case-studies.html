Project overview

  • Industry: Retail / Florist (SMB)
  • Client: J&J Flowers (Florida, USA)
  • Duration: 3 months
  • Website: jandjflowers.com

J&J Flowers — Automation & Digitalization Consulting

Challenge

High operational costs, manual order preparation, inefficient delivery coordination, and no structured CRM or marketing approach.

Solution

Streamlined the sales and order process, optimized preparation and delivery workflows, implemented CRM for customer and order tracking, and defined cost-effective local and online marketing practices.

Results

Reduced order preparation time by ~25–30%, improved delivery consistency, centralized customer/order data, and increased local visibility and repeat customers.
